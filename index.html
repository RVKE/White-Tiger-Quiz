THE WHITE TIGER

   

What is The White Tiger about?

The White Tiger follows a darkly comic Bangalore driver through the poverty and corruption of modern India’s caste society. Through Balrams eyes, Aravind Adiga writes about the dark underbelly of Modern India and does so with a punch.

Who is Balram exactly?

Balram is a poor Indian villager whose great ambition leads him to the zenith of Indian business culture, the world of the Bangalore entrepreneur. On the occasion of the president of China’s impending trip to Bangalore, Balram writes a letter to him describing his transformation and his experience as driver and servant to a wealthy Indian family, which he thinks exemplifies the contradictions and complications of Indian society.  
Can you bring light in your life by making dark choices? Balram makes his own rules.
